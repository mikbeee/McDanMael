About Fleek

Fleek is a free, incentivized Notes-Sharing website. McDanMael Jeelan started this project at an impromptu meeting in a vacant lair at the Columbia/Barnard Hillel building. Our goal is to make your college experience the best that it can be, and to slowly make the world a better place.

WE WANT YOUR NOTES. And you want ours. Sign up for Fleek. You'll be glad you did ;)

(not to be confused with Fleek Eyebrows, LLC - the startup created by internet Vine sensation Mikaella "My-brows-be-fleek" Bernstein.

Feel free to contact us with any feedback, questions, or concerns at NotesOnFleek@gmail.com
